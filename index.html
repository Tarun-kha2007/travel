How to Use RailYatra

Booking Your Train Journey

  1. Fill in the train number (you can find this on IRCTC or railway websites)
  2. Enter your source and destination stations
  3. Select your journey date
  4. Choose your travel class (1A, 2A, 3A, SL, etc.)
  5. Select any applicable concession or quota type (General, Tatkal, Premium Tatkal, etc.)
  6. Add passenger details including:
    • Name, age, and gender
    • Government ID information
    • Individual food preferences
    • Individual berth preferences
  7. Provide your contact number for updates
  8. Click "Submit Booking Request"
  9. You will receive a confirmation message on WhatsApp

After Booking

  • Our team will process your request within 24 hours
  • You'll receive PNR details and payment link via WhatsApp/SMS
  • Complete payment to confirm your tickets
  • E-ticket will be sent to your registered contact

Important Notes

  • All fields marked with * are mandatory
  • Ensure government ID details match exactly with your ID proof
  • Tatkal bookings open at 10 AM one day before journey
  • Premium Tatkal fares are dynamic and may be higher
  • For group bookings, add all passengers before submitting
  • Ticket confirmation is subject to availability
